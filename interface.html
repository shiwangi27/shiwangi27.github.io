Good aspects of the Design

The good aspects of the design is having a nice gradient for temperature control, can control the rate of flow of water, switch between shower/bath, two language supports, user can have waether information and see time while in shower and can change background images.
Besides this, I have tried to make it look attractive and made it easy to use by just putting everything on the screen and enabling and disabling the options.
I have also taken into account the reach of controls by the user while using the shower.
