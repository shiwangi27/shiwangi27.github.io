Shower the People

About the design

Sequence of Actions

Go to the shower, open the shower curtain. If you want to take a bath, press the diverter down if it's up for the water to fill before which you need to make sure if the water temperature is precisely what you desire by rotating the handle. You would want to press the button at the bottom of the tub so that it fills up for a comfortable hot (or warm) bath.

If you want to take a shower, pull the diverter up if it's down and then control the water temperature for a good shower. In this design, you can control the water temperature by rotating the handle. You might also want to adjust the shower head for the reach or angle you want to take shower in.

Lastly you would rotate the handle to stop the water from running once you are done with the shower and also press the button for it close open up the sink to empty the water in the tub.
